Resilience Convergence Poster

Poster for the Resilience Convergence event that features a visualization of the research interests (topic, timescale, and geographic scale) of all the participants at the one-day conference. The intention of this work was to help the attendees, composed of university faculty and industry experts, discover shared interests, find new connections, and recognize the diversity of approaches towards the broad topic of resilience. The data was collected through an online survey that was sent out to participants weeks before the event.
